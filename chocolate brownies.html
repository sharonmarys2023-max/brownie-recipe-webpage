🍫 Chocolate Brownies

Welcome to the ultimate guide for making rich and fudgy chocolate brownies! These treats are crisp on the edges and soft in the center.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/3 cup cocoa powder
  • 1/2 cup flour
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking powder

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C)
  2. Melt butter and let it cool
  3. Mix butter and sugar
  4. Add eggs and vanilla
  5. Add dry ingredients
  6. Pour into pan
  7. Bake 20–25 minutes
  8. Cool and serve
